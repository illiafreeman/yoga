Instructors

Kate White
Kate holds the Yoga Alliance's highest level E-RYT 500 certification and YACEP accreditation. She received her RYT 500-hour certification from the Kripalu Center for Yoga & Health, her RYT 200-hour certification from YogaWorks, and her RYT 200-hour certification from Zarnay Yoga. Kate also passed the industry's most rigorous YogaWorks Certification Assessment, and is a YogaWorks Yoga Teacher Trainer leading RYT 200-hour Training Programs at YogaBalance. Kate teaches Restorative Yoga, Gentle Yoga, Meditation, Align and Flow classes. She focuses on mindful alignment, intelligent class sequencing, and using the breath to help students tap into their subtle energy and create a state of inner calm and ease. Kate is also the Director of YogaBalance, where her tenacity and business acumen have helped create one of New England's top yoga studios.

Anna Zorzou
"The deeper you approach the understanding of human nature, let alone your self, the more you realize that this process is endless." Certified 500-hour YogaWorks Teacher Head of YogaWorks 200 and 300 hours of teacher training programs in Greece, Cyprus and Europe Teacher of prenatal yoga, postnatal yoga, birthlight (yoga for infants) and yoga therapist Yoga Teacher of the Women's National Basketball Team in Greece Yoga Advisor at Holmes Place Clubs (Greece) Ambassador of Manduka Since I remember myself, I've always been an adventurous girl, full of ingenuous curiosity and energy. My desire for new experiences led me to California, where I studied with a full scholarship as a basketball at the University of the Pacific College, Stockton, leaving for a few years my birthplace, Greece. After my studies and unfortunately after having suffered numerous injuries, I started to look for something new to replace basketball, and to help my body retreat. My first yoga lesson was at YogaWorks Main Street studio with Annie Carpenter and it was a love thunder. I immediately became an enthusiastic trainee, following one lesson after another and gaining new experiences. Soon I successfully completed the 200-hour YogaWorks teacher training. There were many more 200 hours of training, workshops, seminars and retreats, as well as an additional 300 hours of therapeutic yoga at the Kripalu Integrative Yoga Therapy Center with Joseph Le Page. Since then, my love for yoga has become stronger. Life brought me back to Greece, close to my family and loved ones, old and new friends. As a teacher of YogaWorks I am head of the 200 and 300 hours program in Greece, Cyprus and Europe, I teach numerous retreats like "Santorini Loves Yoga" with Ilaengar Yoga Junior Intermediate III and participate in similar conferences throughout the world. Every day, I feel grateful and respectful for this beautiful journey of my life, but also enthusiasm for what's about to come. Learn more at annazorzou.com.
