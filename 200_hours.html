Our Experienced Faculty is Here to Guide You

YogaWorks instructors are expertly-trained, impassioned educators who make yoga accessible to every body.

They’ve been featured in international publications and programs, led sold out workshops and retreats, and shared their love of yoga in destinations near and far.

No matter which instructor you have at YogaWorks, you’ll be alongside an industry-leading yoga trainer who’s ready to simplify your learning process. Each member of our widely-respected training team has hundreds, if not thousands, of hours of training, and you’ll be led by and learn from their immense wellspring of expertise. YogaWorks trainers are well-studied in all of the topics covered in the 200-Hour training and have the skills to structure, organize and teach you how to teach others. In addition our trainers are highly skilled in the online teacher training experience. Think of your trainer as your personal coach, guiding you through every detail as it unfolds, so you can train with confidence and ease.
